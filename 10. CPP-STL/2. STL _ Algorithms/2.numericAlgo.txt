#include<numeric>

Numeric Algorithms:


1. std::accumulate: Computes the sum of elements in a range.

2. std::inner_product: Computes the inner product (dot product) of two ranges.

3. std::partial_sum: Computes the partial sums of a range (e.g., cumulative sums).

4. std::iota: Fills a range with sequentially incrementing values (e.g., 0, 1, 2, ...).
